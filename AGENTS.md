# Repository Instructions

This repository is intended to become public. Keep all text externally readable, technically precise, and free of AI attribution.

## Safety Boundary

- Do not add operational chemical or biological harm instructions.
- Do not include full-text harmful prompts in examples, tests, fixtures, or documentation.
- Use redacted summaries for disallowed cases.
- Keep classifier features high level unless a specific private review requires more detail.
- Prefer safety policy, evaluation, and calibration language over procedural domain content.

## Writing

- No em dashes.
- No fabricated statistics or citations.
- Verify external URLs before adding them.
- Use domain-correct language and avoid promotional phrasing.
